Dudhsagar Falls

Dudhsagar Falls is a four-tiered waterfall on the Mandovi River in the Indian state of Goa. It is 60 km from Panaji by road and is located on the BelgaumVasco Da Gama rail route about 46 km east of Madgaon and 80 km south of Belgaum. Dudhsagar Falls is amongst India's tallest waterfalls with a height of 310 m and an average width of 30 metres.

Nohkalikai Falls

Nohkalikai Falls is the tallest plunge waterfall in India. Its height is 340 metres The waterfall is located in the Indian state of Meghalaya, near Cherapunji. Nohkalikai Falls are fed by the rainwater collected on the summit of a comparatively small plateau and decrease in power during the dry season

Kunchikal Falls

Kunchikal Falls is a waterfall in India located in the Nidagodu village near Masthikatte in the Shimoga district of Karnataka state. Kunchikal Falls cascades down rocky boulders and the total height of the falls is 455 meters. Kunchikal Falls is formed by the Varahi river.
